about

I'm a CS (Machine Learning track) and Technology & Information Design double major at the University of Maryland, College Park, minoring in Technology, Entrepreneurship & Corporate Innovation. I transferred in from Prince George's Community College, and I'm on track to graduate in May 2027.

My long-term goal is to become a STEM professor. The plan runs through an MS in CS, a PhD in HCI, and some time in industry as a software engineer or product designer along the way.

My work sits at the intersection of engineering and advocacy. A lot of it is shaped by my own experience as an immigrant, and by wanting to make STEM feel reachable for students who don't usually see themselves reflected in it.
